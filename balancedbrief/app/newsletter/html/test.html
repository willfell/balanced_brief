Discover the Clinically Relevant Cell-Based Activities of Pentadecanoic Acid (C15:0), an Essential Fatty Acid

Pentadecanoic acid (C15:0) is a fatty acid that has been shown to have various health benefits, including protecting heart, immune, and liver health. It activates AMPK and inhibits mTOR, which are important components of the human longevity pathway. In a study comparing C15:0 with three other compounds known to enhance longevity, C15:0 and rapamycin showed the most clinically relevant activities. They shared several beneficial effects, including anti-inflammatory, antifibrotic, and anticancer activities. These findings support the idea that C15:0 is an essential nutrient with similar or even greater health benefits than other longevity-enhancing compounds.
